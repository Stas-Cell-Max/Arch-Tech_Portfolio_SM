OPP Modernization

Ontario 2017-2018 | Architect of Record: NORR | Project Type: Institutional | Schematic Design - Design Development - Construction Documents | Technology: Revit, Bluebeam

Contribution:

  • As a part of a team, designed nine new community police detachment facilities for the OPP.
  • Prepared key site and parcel diagrams, addressing site statistics and phasing plans to outline project scope and logistical considerations.
  • Created preliminary elevation and sectional drawings for each building, focusing on the visual and structural integration of elements across the site to meet design and zoning requirements.
  • Developed site plan layouts, detailing building positioning, access routes, and utility connections to ensure zoning compliance and optimal site functionality.
  • Coordinated BIM with consultants (structural, mechanical, electrical) for systems integration.
  • Finalized detailed drawings, specifications, and plans for construction.
  • Included technical information like materials, dimensions, and structural details.
  • Generated a full set of construction documents required for permits and contractor bidding.
